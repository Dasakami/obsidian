## Every tense has

- **Statement (+)** ‚Äî —É—Ç–≤–µ—Ä–∂–¥–µ–Ω–∏–µ

## Key Tips

- Focus on **FORM first**, TIME comes naturally.
    
- If you know **which auxiliary to use**, you can make **any question or negative**.
    
- Verb form after auxiliary ‚Üí **always base form** (V1), except for continuous/perfect tenses where the verb changes.
